Welcome to Chonky Woodworks

At Chonky Woodworks, we create handcrafted pieces designed to bring beauty and functionality into your home. From stunning cutting boards and cheese boards to elegant wooden and epoxy side tables, each item is crafted with care, precision, and an eye for detail. Let us help you add a touch of timeless charm to your space!

Cutting Boards

Our cutting boards are crafted from premium hardwoods like walnut, maple, purple heart, and padauk, ensuring both durability and timeless beauty. Perfect for daily use or as a standout centerpiece, each board is designed to elevate your kitchen experience.

Epoxy Tables

Our side tables combine craftsmanship and high-quality materials to create functional works of art. Made with beautiful hardwoods or striking epoxy accents, these tables add a unique touch to any space.
